# Privacy Policy for Hook

**Last Updated**: November 6, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Hook ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Hook (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information**
- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo (optional)
- Authentication credentials (when using email/password registration)

**Catch Information**
- Fish species
- Weight and length measurements
- Photos of catches
- GPS location coordinates (with your permission)
- Bait, rig, and technique information
- Notes and descriptions
- Date and time of catches

**Social Interactions**
- Comments on posts
- Likes and reactions
- Friend connections
- Messages (if enabled)

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

**Usage Information**
- App features used
- Time spent in the app
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance data

**Location Information**
- GPS coordinates (only when you choose to add location to a catch)
- Approximate location based on IP address

### Information from Third Parties

**Social Media Authentication**
- When you sign up using Google or Facebook, we receive:
  - Name
  - Email address
  - Profile picture
  - User ID from the social platform

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Provide and Improve Services
- Create and manage your account
- Enable you to log and track your catches

- Share your catches with other users (based on your privacy settings)
- Provide customer support
- Improve app functionality and user experience

### Social Features
- Connect you with other anglers
- Enable commenting and liking
- Show feed of catches from friends and the community

### Analytics and Research
- Understand how users interact with the app
- Identify bugs and technical issues
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features

### Communications
- Send you app updates and notifications
- Respond to your inquiries
- Send promotional content (with your consent)

### Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our terms of service
- Protect against fraud and abuse

### Deletion

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption**: Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL
- **Authentication**: Secure OAuth 2.0 for social logins
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data
- **Regular Backups**: Secure database backups
- **Monitoring**: Continuous security monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

**Account Data**: Retained until you delete your account
**Catch Logs**: Retained until you delete them or your account
**Analytics Data**: Aggregated and anonymized after 24 months
**Backup Data**: Securely deleted after 90 days

When you delete your account:
- Your profile is permanently deleted
- Your catches and posts are removed
- Comments on others' posts are anonymized
- Backups are purged within 90 days

## Children's Privacy

Hook is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@hook.app, and we will delete such information immediately.

## Location Data

### How We Use Location
- Add GPS coordinates to your catch logs (optional)
- Display catches on an interactive map
- Help you find fishing spots

### Your Control
- Location access is ALWAYS optional
- You can enable/disable location per catch
- Location permissions can be revoked in device settings
- Previously saved locations can be removed

### Location Accuracy
We collect precise GPS coordinates when you choose to add location to a catch. This information is stored with your catch and may be visible to other users if you share the catch publicly.
